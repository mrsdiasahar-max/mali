This Privacy Policy explains how Mali collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our application.

2. Data We Collect

Mali collects only what is necessary to provide the service. We do not collect data we do not need.

A) Data you enter directly:

  • Financial transactions — amounts, categories, dates, descriptions
  • Account balances — cash, bank, and custom accounts
  • Goals, debts, budgets, reminders, and recurring expenses
  • Monthly income and target savings amounts
  • Currency and language preferences

B) Account data (if you create an account):

  • Email address
  • Display name and profile photo (if signing in with Google)
  • Authentication tokens managed by Firebase Auth

C) Usage and technical data (collected automatically):

  • App events and feature usage — via Firebase Analytics
  • Crash reports and error logs — via Firebase Crashlytics
  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • We do not collect your IP address for tracking purposes

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve Mali:

  • To display your financial information within the app
  • To sync your data across your devices via Firebase Firestore
  • To send relevant in-app notifications (reminders, trial expiry)
  • To calculate financial health scores, projections, and simulations
  • To improve app stability and fix bugs using crash reports
  • To understand which features are used most via anonymous analytics

We never use your data for:

  • Advertising or selling to third parties
  • Building profiles for marketing purposes
  • Sharing with banks, financial institutions, or data brokers

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in two places:

  • On your device — locally in the app (app storage / localStorage)
  • In the cloud — in your personal Firebase Firestore collection, accessible only with your account credentials

Firebase is operated by Google LLC and complies with industry-standard security practices including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Your Firestore data is stored in a collection accessible only to your user account — no other Mali user can access your data.

For more information: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
